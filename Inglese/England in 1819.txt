England in 1819

	 // Climax | despised: disprezzato
1.	An old, mad, blind, despised and dying King:
	 // dregs: feccia, dull: ottuso
2.	Princes, the dregs of their dull race, who flow
	 // scorn: disprezzo, spring: sorgenti
3.	Through public scorn - mud from a muddy spring;
	 // neither [...] nor [...] nor [...] : parallelism
4.	Rulers who neither see nor feel nor know,
	 // personification | fainting: svenuto, cling: aggrappato, leechlike: sanguisughe
5.	But leechlike to their fainting Country cling
6.	Till they drop, blind in blood, without blow;
	 // corn laws, popolazione affamata su un campo non colto (di Peeterloo)
7.	A people starved and stabbed on th’ untilled field;
	 // Un esercito che uccide ma è vittima
8.	An army which liberticide and prey
	 // whield: impugnano
9.	Makes as two-edged sword to all who wield;
	 // golden laws: leggi auree | tenta i nobili, e uccide i poveri
10.	Golden and sanguine laws which tempt and slay;
	 // religione sigillata
11.	Religion Christless, Godless - a book sealed;
	 // unrepealed: non rimosso
12.	A senate, Time’s worst statute, unrepealed -
	 // tutte le cose citate prima...
13.	Are graves from which glorious Phantom may
14.	Burst, to illumine our tempestuous day.
