# Henry VIII

So he wanted to annul, using as excuse that the bible said that you can't marry the widow of your own brother. The pope said not (because Catherine of Aragon was related to Charles V)

So in **1534** we have the schism of anglicanism (**the Act of Supremacy**). The monks gone away and the the king sold the monastry.

He married Anne Boleyn, but he gave him a daughter, so he accused her of trason and decapitated her.

He has been married less than a mont later, and she finally got him a boy, **Edward VI**.
