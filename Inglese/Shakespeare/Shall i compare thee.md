Retoric Figures
- Metapphor with the sun
- Personification: Death

This sonnet is addressed to a friend, a patron or a lover, the adresee is addressed directly into the sonnet.

There are 2 adresee, in two parts of the poem;
- The dark lady
	- Passionate love
	- She betrayed him
- Fair Youth
	- Courtly love
	- More romantic
